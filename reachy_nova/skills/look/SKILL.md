# Look Skill

Use your camera to analyze the current scene. Provide a query describing
what you want to know about what you see.

## Parameters
- query (string, required): What to look for or ask about the scene

## Examples
- "who is in front of me"
- "what color is that object"
- "describe the room"
- "how many people do you see"
